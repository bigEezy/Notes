General notes for Markdown  
_italic_ by adding "\_" at the beginning and end of what you want italicized  
**bold** by adding "\*\*" at the beginning and end of what you want in bold  
"#" is used to denote Headings, the number of "#" correlates to the Heading number, i.e. Important! must be a space after the "#"

# Heading 1

## Heading 2

"[ ]" "()" are used to add links  
[Find it on _Google_](http:\www.google.com)

double brackets creates reference links  
[The funnest place on earth!][fun disney]  
[fun disney]: www.disney.com
